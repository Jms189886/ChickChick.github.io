Red Velvet Cake

A red velvet cake is a chocolate cake with a distinctive reddish tone. It is made with flour, sugar, eggs, butter, cocoa, and white vinegar, which gives it its characteristic red color and unique, slightly acidic taste. Paired with cream cheese frosting and can be decorated with swirls of frosting or chocolate chips.
